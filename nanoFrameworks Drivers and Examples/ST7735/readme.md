### ST7735 Driver with Nucleo STM32F411RET6 Pins Class 

|No| ST7735 Pin|Nucleo Pin|
|------|-------|------|
|1|LED   |D7     |
|2|SCK   |SPI2SCK |
|3|SDA   |SPI2MOSI|
|4|A0 (DC)|D9     |
|5|RESET  |D8     |
|6|GND    |GND     |
|7|VCC    |5V     |
